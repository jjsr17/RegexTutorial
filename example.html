Example

Matching a Hex value: /^#?([a-f0-9]{6}|[a-f0-9]{3}$/

  1. / - Beginning of line
  2. # - capturing group no:1
  3. [a-f0-9] matches any chars 'a','f','0','9'
  4. {6} - exactly 6 occurrences
  5. | - or
  6. [a-f0-9] matches any chars 'a','f','0','9'
  7. {3} - exactly 3 occurrences
